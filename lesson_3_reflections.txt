Describe the differences between forks, clones, and branches. When would you 
use one instead of another?

	fork: copy another user's repository, which you can't modify, to your own 
	github account.
	clone: copy a repository in github to your computer
	branch: experiment, add features, fix bugs on your code, 
	without affecting its core
